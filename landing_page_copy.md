# TradeMindAI

## AI customs document review for global trade

TradeMindAI helps importers, exporters, freight forwarders, and customs brokers review shipment documents before customs clearance.

Our assistant checks commercial invoices, packing lists, and shipment details to detect missing information, unclear product descriptions, HS code issues, origin gaps, Incoterms problems, and other risks that can delay clearance.

## What we help with
- Customs document review
- Commercial invoice checks
- Packing list checks
- HS code presence checks
- Country of origin checks
- Incoterms checks
- Import/export clarification questions
- Multilingual trade support

## Who it is for
- Importers
- Exporters
- Customs brokers
- Freight forwarders
- Logistics companies
- E-commerce sellers
- Small manufacturers

## Tagline
Reduce customs document errors before they delay your shipment.

## Main call to action
Send us one shipment file and receive a customs risk review report.

## Compliance note
TradeMindAI is a decision-support tool. Final customs classification, duty calculation, and declarations must be validated by a qualified customs professional.
