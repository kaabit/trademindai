# TradeMindAI Pricing Draft

## Service model first
Start as a human-assisted AI review service before building a full SaaS platform.

### Pilot review
- Price: Free to €49
- One old or anonymized shipment file
- Output: short customs risk review report

### Pay-per-shipment review
- Price: €49–€99 per shipment
- Commercial invoice + packing list review
- Missing fields and risk report
- Clarification questions

### Small business monthly plan
- Price: €199/month
- Up to 5 shipment reviews/month
- Email support
- Monthly summary

### Broker / freight forwarder plan
- Price: €499–€1,500/month
- Higher monthly review volume
- Standardized reports
- Team workflow
- Priority support

### Enterprise setup
- Price: €5,000+ setup + monthly fee
- Custom workflows
- Private deployment option
- API/integration discussion
